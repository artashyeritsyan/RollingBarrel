🍺 RollingBarrel

Rolling Barrel is a small maze game I created where the goal is simple: collect 50 beer mugs 🍺 before time runs out ⏳.

You start inside a maze, and the timer is already ticking. At the top of the screen, a green bar shows how much time you have left. When that bar reaches zero — the run is over.

Beer mugs 🍺 are scattered all around the maze. Every time you collect one, your barrel fills up a little more. You can see your progress in the yellow bar at the top of the screen. Watching it grow feels satisfying — it means you’re getting closer to winning.

But you’re not alone.

A Skeleton 💀 is wandering through the maze, trying to catch you. If it touches you, that’s it — you lose. So while you’re racing against time ⏳ to collect mugs 🍺, you also have to stay alert and avoid it.

You also have a Dash ability ⚡. It helps you dodge the Skeleton 💀 or escape when it gets too close. But it costs beer 🍺. Every time you use Dash ⚡, you lose some of the filled beer in your barrel, and the yellow bar goes down. So you can’t just use it all the time — you have to decide carefully when it’s worth it.

If you collect 50 mugs 🍺 before time runs out ⏳, you win. If the Skeleton 💀 catches you or the timer hits zero, you start again.
